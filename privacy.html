Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 March 2026

Sanctum Vox ("we", "our", "us") operates the Sanctum Vox Interactive Articulator application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind. We do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Recent sounds: A list of recently viewed IPA sounds, stored in your browser's local storage to improve your experience.
  • Theme preference: Your selected light or dark mode preference.

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: Typography is loaded from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may collect standard web request data such as your IP address when these fonts are loaded. See Google Fonts Privacy FAQ.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services of any kind.

4. Audio Files

IPA audio samples are bundled locally within the App. In rare cases where a local file is unavailable, the App may fetch audio from Wikimedia Commons. No personal data is transmitted in these requests.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is an educational tool suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Cookies

The App does not use cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
